You walk along the Python Path until you arrive at Module 1's Home.

You knock on the door. A second later, Module 1 answers. He rubs his eyes.

"Who are you? Oh my, you adventured to the Realm of Modules to become Module Z? That's no simple task. Come in, come in."

You enter.

Module 1 welcomes you inside and leads you to a room lined with picture frames.

"I am an artist. After our journey was complete, I rushed to my studio and drew what I could not explain in words. Please learn what you must from my art."

You walk up to the art wall.
